# Support Policy — SMRITI Retail OS

Thank you for using SMRITI Retail OS. We are committed to providing reliable support for our community and enterprise customers.

---

## Technical Support Channels

### 1. Community Support
For general questions, configuration assistance, and developer discussions:
* **GitHub Discussions:** Use the discussions tab in the repository to communicate with other developers and contributors.
* **Knowledge Base:** Refer to official guides under the `/docs/` directory.

### 2. Commercial & Enterprise Support
For production support, priority bug fixing, custom feature implementation, or integration help:
* **Website:** Visit [https://smritisys.com](https://smritisys.com)
* **Email:** Contact **support@smritisys.com** (primary support channel)

---

## Issue Resolution SLA (Commercial Tier)

For customers with an active enterprise agreement:
* **Critical Issues (Production Outage):** Resolution/mitigation within 4 hours.
* **High Issues (Core feature broken, no workaround):** Resolution/mitigation within 24 hours.
* **Medium Issues (Feature broken, workaround exists):** Resolution/mitigation within 3 business days.
* **Low Issues (Minor bugs, documentation):** Resolution in the next scheduled patch release.

---

## Security Support

If you suspect or discover a security issue, please **do not** open a public GitHub issue. Refer to our [Security Policy](SECURITY.md) and email **security@smritisys.com** immediately.
