# SMP-004: Capability Profiles & Configuration Templates Standard (v1.0)

## 1. Distinction
- **Capability Profiles:** Define *what* modules are enabled (e.g. `Retail Lite v1.0`, `Kirana & Supermarket v1.0`, `Medical Store v1.0`, `Restaurant v1.0`, `Enterprise Chain v1.0`).
- **Configuration Templates:** Define *how* enabled modules behave (e.g. Simple POS layout vs Touch POS layout, Compact Dashboards).

## 2. One-Click Capability Profiles
1. **Retail Lite v1.0:** Sales, Purchase, Inventory, POS, Barcode.
2. **Kirana & Supermarket v1.0:** Retail Lite + Batch, Expiry, GST, Barcode.
3. **Medical Store v1.0:** Retail Lite + Expiry, Batch, GST, Drug License.
4. **Restaurant v1.0:** Retail Lite + KOT, Table Mgmt, Recipe.
5. **Enterprise Chain v1.0:** All 12 Domains Enabled.
